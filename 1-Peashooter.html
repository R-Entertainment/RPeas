A mini table/almanac showcasing the ever-expanding family of pea plants in the sequel of PopCap's most successful game, Plants Vs. Zombies 2.


The Pea Table features the plant's description and four basic information about the plant. The Pea Table
contains 16 pea plants from Plants Vs. Zombies 2 that resemble or behave like the classic Peashooter.




NAVIGATION GUIDE
Click on the navigation buttons below the table to navigate through each plant.
White buttons have no navigation.
